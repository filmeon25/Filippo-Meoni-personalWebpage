My volleyball career

When I started playing:

Before playing volleyball I played a lot of sports, looking for the one that I liked the most.
Before volleyball the one that I loved was basketball, but one summer I wanted to try volleyball
(since my dad was a really good professional volleyball player, with medals in the olympic games).
So I tried volleyball for the first time, and I just fell in love with it.
I played volleyball at a minicamp, where we would go and practice in the morning,
get lunch and, practice again in the afternoon. It was 2 weeks long and I got to know some new
people that still now some of my best friends!

U 13:

          This was my first year playing volleyball, and I didn't know this, but I joined one of the best teams in Italy, and I didn't get to play a lot. I only got to play when we were up by a lot of
points, but since our team was really good, we won our regional championship!

U 14:

This year our team was even better, but since we still had the same team we were even better.
I still didn't get to play too much, but this year was the first cathegory were we can go to
nationals, and we WON!! We were so happy, we couldn't believe we actually won!
I also got to play in some pool play and I got the last point against the team that we also
ended up playing in the finals!

U 16 first year:

This year we had to play with also guys one year older than us becuse U15 doesn't exist in Italy. I got a lot better during this year, and I got a lot more playing time during this year, I also got to play a lot during nationals. Unfortunately, we didn't play good in the quarter finals. We underestimated the team too much, to they point of losing against them. After that game we were depressed and didn't play good at all for the rest of the nationals, ending up finishing 8th in the nation. Even if that is not what we wanted, we still got a really good result!

U 16 second year:

We didn't play really good this year, we didn't qualify for nationals so we didn't get
to play what we wanted to do. But it was a really good year for me since I got to
go to practice in a team that select the best people from the region (in comparison to the
US is like saying states). And I got to practice with them a lot, I almost made the team
that plays a high level torurnament. Every region has their own team and they play against                             
other regions.

U 18 first year:

This was one of my favorite years, for the first half of the season I didn't get
to play a lot, but I was working really hard, and my coach saw that, and when I was
going in for one of the starters I would always do better than him. So, mid-season
he decided to give me an opportunity as a starter, and I killed it! So after that he
kept me in and I was doing good all year, so this was the frist time I managed to
                    get a starting position! Unfortunately this turned out to be my last year with this team. I loved every single one of my teammates, they were like a second family for me. I also got to join our team in a world tournament, were we played against national
teams, and for how good we were we finished second in the world!

Senior year in high school:

When I moved to the US I had to change team and I joined a team called HVA.
I didn't know the guys and we didn't have as many practices as I had with my old                                         
team, so it was hard for me to bond with them. I managed to have a starting spot
on this team too, but when we started season COVID started spreading, so they
canceled our season and we didn't get to play nationals.

First year at Vanguard:

Now I am at Vanguard, here I competed at a really high level volleyball, I didn't know
this yet, but we had one of the best teams in the NAIA. And I got to play as a starter!
Unfortunately, for COVID we didn't get to play too much. We played only one team seven
times for conference, and we won! Then we went to nationals and we managed to upste
the third seed, but we lost in the semifinal, ending up as third.

Second year at Vanguard:

For my second year at Vanguard we had some new players coming in, but we didn't
lose anyone, and since last year we finished third we expected to do really good.
During pre-season we did really good beating some of the best Division 1 teams!
When we started the actual season our main player got injured, but luckily we still
did an amazing job, beating almost every team we played, and also the team that won
against us at nationals the year before! Unfortunately when the guy that got injured
came back we started going downhill. I had a fracture on my shins and still played through
it, and the setter was not making smart decisions, we lost during the conference semifinals
but since we played great during the year we were still able to qualify for nationals
since we were ranked first in the US. At nationals we lost in the quarter finals, so
we got fifth place. Not what we expected/wanted but still a good result.

First year at UT Austin:

This year I started playing for the men's volleyball club at UT, and the level of competition I was used to is not the
same, but I met some amazing people and it is still really fun to play. Our season hasn't started yet, but our team is
looking really good compared to some teams that we played during preseason. In every tournement that we played
we managed to get into the final, except our last tournament, but for a team with three new players it is a huge achievement. I am also a practice player for the women's NCAA D1 team, and they are amazing, we were able to win
the National Championship title!
